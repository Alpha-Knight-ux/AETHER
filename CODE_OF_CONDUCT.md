# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful community for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful
- Being constructive
- Helping other users
- Giving useful feedback
- Respecting different opinions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Personal attacks
- Spam
- Sharing malicious software
- Posting illegal content

## Enforcement

The project owner reserves the right to remove comments, discussions, or users that violate this Code of Conduct.

Let's work together to build a friendly and helpful community around AETHER.
